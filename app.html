Printing advice

We know it’s not always easy to get printing working on your phone, tablet or even your desktop! Here’s some advice to give you a helping hand:

  • Try saving for printing later, either choose to print as a PDF or use our save button.
  • Some printers allow you to email attachments to them for printing, you just need the address.
  • As a last resort you can save the preview image in the previous menu (right click or touch-hold to save). It may look small but it's actually full size.
  • Any questions please email amoles@toucanbox.com
